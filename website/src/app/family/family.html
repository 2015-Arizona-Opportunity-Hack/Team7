Family Resource Center

The Family Resource Center provides a safe, positive environment for family development, designed to meet families' needs beyond nutrition.

New Directions Institute – Getting Your Child’s Brain Ready for School; please call for more information or to schedule an appointment.

Touch of Love Ministries -Fellowship meal open to any and all on the last Saturday of the month – November 24th between 3:00 and 5:00 pm.

Dignity Health – children ages 0-5 during supplemental food distribution – November 30th between 7:30 and 10:00 am.
