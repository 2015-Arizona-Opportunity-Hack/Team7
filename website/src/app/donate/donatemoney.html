Charitable Tax Credit

Arizona taxpayers who contribute to CCCC may receive a dollar-for-dollar tax credit. Because CCCC provides assistance to the working poor, Arizona state law allows taxpayers who contribute to CCCC to take a tax credit for up to $400 for couples filing jointly and $200 for single taxpayers. The last day to make contributions to count for the current year is December 31st. Please consult your tax advisor for additional information.

Thank you for your donations and support

The organization is a 501(c)(3) non-profit corporation and gratefully accepts financial contributions, as well as contributions of in-kind goods and services. Monetary donations may qualify under the Helping the Working Poor tax credit. All donations are fully tax deductible as allowed by law. Receipts are provided at time of drop off. Checks / money orders mailed to our PO Box (P.O. Box 591 Chandler, AZ 85244) receive a letter acknowledging donation and providing the FEIN number.
